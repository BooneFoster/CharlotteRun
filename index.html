Welcome to Charlotte Run

We are your local page for all things Charlotte running. Make sure check our pages out to log your running miles through our system and we can build our community through collective miles. Check the events tab to get involved in races and other things happening. Head over to the gallery to see our community in action, and finally check out our local weekly charity!

Meet the Founders


My name is Bill Foster and my name is Boone Foster and we are the proud founders of Charlotte Run! As runners in the Charlotte area ourselves we saw an opportunity to begin to better connect our fellow runners. Our goal is to continue to grow this awesome community of runners. We truly hope to see you at our events or logging miles. Finally, enjoy the page and please feel free to reach out.
